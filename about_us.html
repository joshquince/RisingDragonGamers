ABOUT US

Welcome to Rising Dragon (RD) Gamers, we are a gaming group based in Bathurst and enjoy anything related to games. We currently meet on a weekly basis and boast a group of round 20 people

Who Can Play

Anyone from the ages of 13 and up. We accept anyone regardless of ability level or gender. Just come and have fun!

Where we Meet

We meet every Wednesday afternoons from 6pm to 10pm at 2 Dry Street, Bathurst, NSW, 2795
